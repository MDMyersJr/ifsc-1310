Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

Progressive enhancement is a design philosophy centered around the content. Properly marking up your content with sound HTML gives a better user experience with or without the additional layers you may add. Some users may be unable to access the CSS or JavaScript you've written. In an article by Jason Knight, they mention that HTML is for more than just users sitting at a screen. You want your website to be able to relay the content properly in a wide array of use cases. When designing a website with progressive enhancement in mind, you create a more accessible and bulletproof result.
